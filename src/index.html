In lands forsaken by light, where silence weaves through crumbling halls,
a lone soul, burdened yet unbroken, dared to shape the unknown.

Thus was the Tarnished House born—
a realm wrought from fading memory and undying will.
May those who enter seek purpose, and find it wanting.
